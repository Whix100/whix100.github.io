Whixmas Advent Calendar

During the month of December, I release a series of 10 videos called the '10 Days of Whixmas'. Those videos lead up to December 25th also known as Whixmas. No one except those who have worked on the video know what each day is until the day is released. This year I have some fun and exciting surprises ready for the 10 Days of Whixmas. If you would like to be notified of each video in Discord, check out Titan, my multipurpose Discord bot. Then in the server you want notifications in, type 't!sub whixmas'.
